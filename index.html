Our Service

Site survey to identify energy saving opportunities

An energy expert will visit your site to determine scope for energy savings.

An IoT system will be installed at your facility to give further insights on how major loads are performing.

You may view your facility’s energy consumption data here.

Presentation of energy saving measures

  • Next, we’ll give you a detailed report outlining energy saving measures you could implement.
  • The report will include payback period, cost of measures, etc
  • We’ll present to you saving opportunities that best match your investment/payback period expectations.

Implementation

  • We also assist with implementation.

Monitoring savings

  • The end goal is to ensure you’re generating savings, and thereby lowering your facility’s carbon footprint.
  • Our IoT system accurately monitors savings, so you can be sure that you’ll be paying lower utility costs. We’re open to collecting our service fee from savings that you’ll generate, and not beforehand.
  • Also, you may choose to invest some of the savings in newer, state-of-the art energy technologies in order to further reduce utility costs.
